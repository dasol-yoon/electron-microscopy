## Tomography holder

**Unload sample**

1. The sample grid is held between the two "arms" of the metal strips.

   <img src="img/tomography/01-sample-loaded.jpg" alt="Sample grid held by metal strips" width="500">

2. Rotate the screw counterclockwise about 90 degrees. Repeat for the other "arm".

   <img src="img/tomography/02-rotate-screw.jpg" alt="Rotating screw counterclockwise" width="500">

3. Move the two strips away from each other to free the sample

   From:

   <img src="img/tomography/03-strips-closed.jpg" alt="Strips close together" width="500">

   To:

   <img src="img/tomography/04-strips-open.jpg" alt="Strips moved apart" width="500">

4. Remove the sample

   <img src="img/tomography/05-sample-removed.jpg" alt="Sample removed" width="500">

**Load sample**

Follow the process in reverse: load a new sample, pull the two strips closer together, and rotate the screws clockwise.
